United India's Motor Policy

Car insurance provides the financial cover you need at the time of an accident.
While driving, especially if you are alone in an emergency, the right cover is precisely what you need. It also lends you peace of mind at the same time.
When you buy a car insurance policy from Beema Dukaan, you get more than basic protection. With it, car owners like you are covered against the below crucial factors:
  • Damages to the insured vehicle.
  • Any third-party liability determined by the law.

What does United India Motor Policy Cover ?

The policy offers the following covers :
  • Section 1 : Undertakes to reimburse expenses incurred for.
    A) Repair / replacement of the damaged parts of the vehicle , due to accident.
    B) To pay the insured declared value (IDV) in case of a total loss where the cause of such damage is an accident or theft.
  • Section 2 : This is compulsory. It covers the legal liability to third party personal injury or death and property damage arising out of an accident involving the insured vehicle.

Other covers without any extra cost :

  • Damage to vehicle whilst in transit by air, road, rail, inland waterway, lift or elevator.
  • Whatever “No Claim Discount” earned in your current vehicle can be carried over to a new vehicle of the same class that you may purchase, within a period of three years.

What additional covers can be opted for by payment of extra premium ?

  • Loss of accessories.
  • Legal liability to the paid driver, cleaner or workman.
  • Personal accident to occupants.
  • Personal Accident for owner driver is compulsory.
  • Increased legal liability towards property damage of third party up to a limit of Rs. 7.5 lacs.

Inclusion, Exclusion & Additional Covers

  • Add-on covers to Motor Policy !
    1. Nil Depreciation cover for private car / Commercial vehicle - Depreciation is not deducted in replacement of parts for partial loss claims.
    2. Courtesy Car - for private cars - reimbursement for hire of rental vehicles when insured car is in garage for repairs or car is stolen ( maximum 7 days, twice in a year. )
    3. Medical Expenses - Private cars - expenses incurred on hospitalization of occupants of car arising out of road accidents subject to limits.
    4. Personal Effects - Cover will reimburse loss of personal effects of the insured provided there is a valid claim under the OD section of the policy not exceeding the sum insured opted by the insured.
  • What does the policy not cover?
    Your four-wheeler insurance excludes the following:
    1. Any accidents outside the geographical area.
    2. Consequential loss, normal wear and tear.
    3. Driving without a valid license for that class of vehicle
    4. Driving under the influence of alcohol / drugs and accidents occur.
    5. Vehicle not being used as per limitations of use.
    6. Depreciation for that parts that needs replacements in the event of accident is pre-defined in the policy.
    Whether you buy car insurance online or offline, you need to know about these inclusions and exclusions.
  • Additional Covers from Beema Dukaan
    When you buy car insurance, you can also opt for add-on covers based on your needs. Let's take a look:
    a) Depreciation Cover
    Under this cover, you can claim the depreciation amount deducted on the value of parts. These are parts you may have replaced as a result of an admissible claim under the Own Damage Section of your policy.
    Conditions Under Which You Can Claim this Cover:
    You have to repair the insured vehicle at any of Beema Dukaan's authorised garages.
    Exclusions Under this Cover:
    You cannot claim repairs at any of our authorised garages.

FAQ's About Car Insurance

  • Is it mandatory to have car insurance in India?​
    Yes, motor insurance is mandatory in India. You have to have at least a third-party car insurance policy in India. It is a statutory requirement as per the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988.
  • What is car insurance?
    It is an insurance policy that protects cars against damage or loss resulting from an accident, theft or any other third-party liability. You can buy car insurance online or by visiting a Beema Dukaan branch.
  • When do you buy a commercial car insurance policy?
    A commercial car insurance policy is required if you use or operate a commercial vehicle up to 3500 GVW. For example, a black and yellow taxi.
  • Why purchase car insurance online?
    It is beneficial to buy a car policy online in India, as there are various policies from which to choose. You can make more informed decisions. When you buy any car policy online, research thoroughly and then decide. Buying online also helps you save time and money. You can also get more benefits.
  • What are the types of car policies offered by Beema Dukaan?
    Beema Dukaan offers two types of car insurance- a Package Policy and a Third Party Liability Policy.
  • How can I pay for car insurance online?
    There are multiple online payment options, e.g. credit card, net banking, debit card, etc.
