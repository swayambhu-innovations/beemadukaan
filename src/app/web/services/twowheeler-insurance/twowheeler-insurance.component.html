Why Should You Have Two Wheeler Insurance?

There are five reasons why you must have two wheeler insurance:
  • It Is Mandatory: The most prominent reason to have a two wheeler policy is that you have a legal obligation to do so. In India, driving a two wheeler without third-party insurance is a punishable offence. This rule is according to the terms of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988.
  • It Helps Pay for Damages: Have you ever noticed minor scratches or abrasions on your two wheeler? Has your two wheeler's side-view mirror been knocked off because someone threw a cricket ball at it? All these and more damages may occur whether your vehicle is moving or parked. With a robust two wheeler policy, you can claim damages and cut down on repair expenses.
  • It Protects Your Loved Ones When You Can't: Two Wheeler accidents happen without warning, and at times they may be serious or fatal. Your injuries may cause partial or permanent disability or even death. If you want your family's finances to be secure in case anything happens to you while driving, then you must buy two wheeler insurance. To safeguard yourself you can opt for an owner-driver cover on your policy.

Types of two wheeler insurance covers

At Beema Dukaan, you can choose between two types of two wheeler policies. You can visit the nearest branch; however, if you buy two wheeler insurance online, it is more convenient and faster. Take a look at both policy options:
  • Liability Only Policy: Having this two wheeler insurance policy is a statutory requirement in India. Any damage or theft to your vehicle by a third party gets covered.
  • Stand-Alone Own Damage (SOD) Policy An SOD or OD insurance cover will cover any damage to your own vehicle not because of a third-party. For example, when your two wheeler is parked, and a tree branch falls on it.
  • Comprehensive Policy: At Liberty General, you can get this as a package. (Liability Only Policy + OD or Own Damage Cover. The cover extends to the owner's vehicle.)
  • Add-On Covers: You can customise and get two wheeler insurance covers according to specific coverage.
    *Add-on covers vary based on how old your vehicle is. Do check with your insurance provider for more information.

Inclusion, Exclusion & Additional Covers

  • What Does Your 4-Wheeler Insurance Include?
    Two Wheeler policy covers have inclusions and exclusions, here is our list of both. At Liberty General, our two wheeler insurance includes own damage to the vehicle insured caused by:
    1. Accident due to external impact
    2. Damage due to an earthquake, flood, storm, landslide, rockslide
    3. Loss of the vehicle through burglary or housebreaking
    4. Irreparable damage of the vehicle due to a fire, explosion, self-ignition, lightning
    5. Damage due to malicious intent including riots, strikes, or terrorism
    6. Accident to the vehicle while in transit by road, rail, inland waterway or on a lift.
  • What are the exclusions in a two wheeler insurance policy?
    Your Liberty General four-wheeler insurance excludes the following:
    1. Wear and tear from daily commute, mechanical or electrical breakdown, failure or breakage
    2. Damage caused by a person driving with an invalid driving license or under the influence of alcohol/drugs
    3. Loss as a consequence of depreciation in your two wheeler's value
    4. Claims arising out of contractual liability
    5. Loss/damage to your two wheeler due to war, civil war, mutiny, nuclear perils, etc.
    6. Damage to tyres and tubes unless it is during an accident
    7. Driving under conditions other than under 'limitations of use'. (E.g. Using a private two wheeler as a cab.)
    8. Loss/damage of your vehicle outside India.
    Whether you buy two wheeler insurance online or offline, you need to know about these inclusions and exclusions.

FAQ's About Two Wheeler Insurance

  • Is it mandatory to have two wheeler insurance in India?​
    Yes, motor insurance is mandatory in India. You have to have at least a third-party two wheeler insurance policy in India. It is a statutory requirement as per the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988.
  • What is two wheeler insurance?
    It is an insurance policy that protects two wheelers against damage or loss resulting from an accident, theft or any other third-party liability. You can buy two wheeler insurance online or by visiting a Liberty General Insurance branch.
  • When do you buy a commercial two wheeler insurance policy?
    A commercial two wheeler insurance policy is required if you use or operate a commercial vehicle up to 3500 GVW. For example, a black and yellow taxi.
  • Why purchase two wheeler insurance online?
    It is beneficial to buy a two wheeler policy online in India, as there are various policies from which to choose. You can make more informed decisions. When you buy any two wheeler policy online, research thoroughly and then decide. Buying online also helps you save time and money. You can also get more benefits.
  • What are the types of two wheeler policies offered by Beema Dukaan?
    Beema Dukaan offers two types of two wheeler insurance- a Package Policy and a Third Party Liability Policy.
  • How can I pay for two wheeler insurance online?
    There are multiple online payment options, e.g. credit card, net banking, debit card, etc.
