Top Up policy

Super Top Up Policy covers In-Patient Hospitalization Expenses.

  • Hospitalisation Expenses incurred, for one or more covered Hospitalisations in the aggregate, during the policy period are covered in excess of a specified level called Threshold Level selected by the proposer up to the Sum Insured chosen. Thus, this policy also gives protection when the total of many Hospitalisation expenses exceeds the Threshold Level even though expenses for each Hospitalisation is below the same. When the Threshold Level has been exhausted by previous covered Hospitalisation expenses, this policy will step in for each subsequent Hospitalisation.
  • The Policy gives additional protection at an affordable price for high sums insured upto a maximum of Z15 lacs above the Threshold Level. This is irrespective of whether the Insured has any other policy or not.
  • If the expenses in excess of Threshold Level are received / reimbursable from any other source whatsoever, the sum insured under this policy will be available to the full extent beyond the amount so received / reimbursable. Thus, this policy offers protection in excess of any Primary Health Policy/Benefit Scheme that the insured may have.
  • When Super Top Up Policy is taken in combination with any other regular Health Policy, total coverage can be as high as! 20 lacs which is not otherwise available.
  • Further the premium for such a combination would be cheaper than for a regular Health Policy for the total sum insured under both policies.
